Welcome to

Cloverville

Cloverville is a small ecological village where residents live sustainably, grow their own food, and share resources.

Our Story

Cloverville started in 2015 as a bold experiment by Green Bob. Tired of the hustle of big cities, he purchased an old farm and decided to transform it into a self-sustaining paradise.

The philosophy is simple: "Give back to nature more than you take." Residents work together to build solar panels, plant community gardens, and ensure everyone contributes fairly via the Green Points system.

About Us & Goals

We are a community of 42 residents dedicated to living in harmony with nature. Our daily life revolves around shared responsibilities and green initiatives.

  • Reduce carbon footprint by 50%
  • Self-sufficiency in food production
  • Zero-waste community
  • Education and green workshops
